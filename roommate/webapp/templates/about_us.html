About Us

Our Mission

Roommate is dedicated to providing students, professionals, and anyone looking for accommodations in Dhaka with an easy, secure, and user-friendly platform. Whether you're looking for a temporary stay or a long-term rental, Roommate makes it simple to connect with others who are offering or seeking housing options.

Who We Are

Roommate was created by a group of passionate individuals who understand the difficulties of finding reliable accommodation in a busy city like Dhaka. The platform is designed to be a trusted hub for students, travelers, and professionals who need a place to stay, as well as a marketplace for landlords and property owners looking to find new tenants.

Our Team
