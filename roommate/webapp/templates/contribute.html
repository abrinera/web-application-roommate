Thank You for Your Interest in Contributing!

At Roommate, we believe in building a community-driven platform where everyone can contribute to making the experience better for all users. Whether you're a developer, a user who has great ideas, or someone who wants to volunteer their time, there are several ways you can get involved!

Ways You Can Contribute

  • Report Bugs: If you've noticed any bugs or glitches while using Roommate, let us know! Your feedback helps us improve the platform.
  • Suggest Features: Have an idea for a new feature or improvement? We welcome suggestions to make Roommate even better for our users.
  • Volunteer: If you're interested in contributing your time or skills (e.g., marketing, social media, community engagement), we would love to hear from you.
